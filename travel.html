Friends That Travel

One app for your whole trip. Itinerary, expenses, maps, and settlement — shared in real time with your group.

What it does

Shared itinerary

Calendar-first view of every activity, transit leg, meal, and hotel. Everyone sees the same plan in real time.

Expense splitting

Log shared and personal expenses as you go. Group-based splitting so costs land in the right place.

Places & maps

Pin hotels, restaurants, and points of interest. See everything on a map with one tap.

End-of-trip settlement

Who owes who, calculated automatically. Payment app deep links make settling up one tap.

Expense reports

Generate a PDF expense report with receipt images. Share via AirDrop, email, or anywhere.

Time zone aware

Crossing time zones? Every event knows what time zone it's in. No more mental math on international trips.

Real time

Changes sync instantly across everyone's phone via Firebase — no refresh needed.

iOS only

Built native for iPhone with Sign in with Apple. Private, simple, and fast.

Currently in beta

Friends That Travel is invite-only while we refine the experience with our test group. Public launch coming in the future.
